Who we are

Welcome to Mission of Faith, where we gather as a community of believers dedicated to spreading the love and message of Jesus Christ. We believe in the power of faith, love and prayer to transform lives and communities. Our mission is to glorify God through worship, fellowship, discipleship, and service.


Founded on the teachings of the Bible, we strive to create an inclusive and welcoming environment where individuals and families can grow spiritually and find support on their journey of faith. Whether you're exploring Christianity for the first time or looking for a spiritual home, we invite you to join us as we seek to follow Jesus and make a positive impact in our local community and beyond.

Meet our Pastors

Meet Pastors Marcus Marinho and his wife Cleide, the dynamic husband-and-wife duo who lead our church family with unwavering dedication and passion. Together, they bring a wealth of experience, compassion, and wisdom to our community.


Pastor's Marcus's journey in ministry began in 2002 when they felt the call to serve God and His people. Alongside them, Pastor Cleide also felt the same divine calling, and together, they embarked on a shared mission to spread the love and message of Jesus Christ.


Through years of study, prayer, and service, Pastors Marcus and Cleide have cultivated a deep understanding of Scripture and a compassionate approach to ministry. Their leadership is characterized by humility, grace, and a genuine desire to see lives transformed by the power of God's Word.


Beyond their pastoral duties, they are actively involved in various community outreach programs, demonstrating our church's commitment to serving others and making a positive impact in our neighborhood. Together, they lead by example, nurturing a welcoming and inclusive church environment where individuals and families can grow spiritually and find support on their journey of faith.

Weekly Program

Study Wednesday

Join us every Wednesday evening for an enriching Bible study experience. This weekly gathering is an opportunity for us to dive deeper into the Scriptures, explore key theological concepts, and grow in our relationship with God. Led by our knowledgeable and passionate leaders, our Wednesday Bible study sessions provide a supportive and engaging environment where participants can ask questions, share insights, and engage in meaningful discussions. Whether you're a seasoned Bible scholar or just beginning to explore the teachings of Christianity, you'll find a welcoming community and a wealth of resources to help you on your spiritual journey.

Youth Friday

Every Friday evening, teens and young adults gather to connect, grow, and explore their faith together. It's a place where friendships are formed, laughter is shared, and hearts are lifted in prayer. Led by dedicated mentors and youth leaders, our Youth Group provides a safe and welcoming space for young people to navigate the challenges of adolescence and deepen their understanding of God's Word. Whether you're seeking fellowship, spiritual growth, or simply a fun place to hang out with friends, you'll find it all at our Friday Youth Group.

Worship Sunday

Our Sunday family sermon is the cornerstone of our weekly worship experience. Each Sunday morning, families come together to lift their voices in praise, receive biblical teaching, and deepen their connection with God and one another. Led by our dedicated pastoral team, the sermon is a time for us to draw near to God, seek His guidance, and be transformed by His Word. With messages rooted in Scripture and relevant to our daily lives, the Sunday sermon provides spiritual nourishment and practical insights that equip us to live out our faith with joy and authenticity.

Sunday School

Sunday Children's School is a vibrant program that runs concurrently with our Sunday worship service. While parents have the opportunity to deepen their knowledge of Scripture and engage in worship, our dedicated team of teachers and volunteers leads our children in age-appropriate Bible lessons, activities, and crafts. Sunday Children's School not only provides a valuable break for parents but also ensures that our youngest members are given the opportunity to learn about God's love and truth in a way that is engaging and relevant to their lives. It's a time for our children to grow in their understanding of Scripture, build lasting friendships, and experience the joy of discovering God's plan for their lives. Join us this Sunday and entrust your children to our caring and capable team as we journey together in faith.
